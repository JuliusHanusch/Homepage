Interests and Experiences

Musical Stations

Julius Hanuschs started playing the trumpet when he was 6 Year old. In his first years he was thaught in the music school of the district of meissen. After 6 Years and several successes at the competition "Jugend Musiziert" he went to the "Landesgymnasium für Musik" in Dresden.
In Dresden Julius Hanuschs received lessos in Piano, Choir and Orchestra. From 2018 to 2019 he was a member of the "Landesjugendorchestrer Sachsen"
Since October 2020 Julius Hanusch is a member of the Universitätsorchester Dresden.
